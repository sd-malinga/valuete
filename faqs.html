FAQ

How does Valuete compare to other crypto-backed lending platforms?

At Valuete our core focus is on security and investor protection, we use advanced multi-sig technology to ensure that the collateral ‘Bitcoin’ is kept incredibly safe. While at the same time, we only work with loans denominated in US dollars that are secured by legal contracts which ensure investor protection.

What does Peer-to-peer Marketplace mean?

On the Valuete marketplace, the borrowing takes place directly between a lender of interest and borrower of interest with Valuete simply acting as an intermediary. Valuete is not involved in loan organization or setting the terms of the loan, but offers tools to facilitate this process between borrowers and lenders.

How does Valuete offer better security?

The Valuete model relies on using 2-of-3 multi-signature wallets for storing Bitcoin which is used as collateral for securing the loan originated between borrower and lender.

What is a Multi-Sig wallet ?

A Multi-Signature, or Multi-Sig, bitcoin wallet has three associated private keys instead of just one. With this configuration, at least 2-of-3 private keys are needed to perform a transaction.

How are Multi-Sig wallets better than the centralized wallets used by others platforms?

Using a Multi-Sig wallet provides a level of security and legal clarity currently not offered by any other bitcoin lending platform. Valuete is not in the business of managing your private keys and creating the risks customers experience using centralized platforms. As the saying goes, “not your keys, not your crypto.”

How much fee does Valuete charge?

We just charge 0.5% of the loan amount from borrowers only lenders don't have to pay any fees for using our platform. Our fees are much lesser compared to other platforms as we don’t have huge overhead costs of the centralized crypto lending platform which we pass on as benefit in form of lower lending costs.

Why are you a Bitcoin-only platform?

Valuete is focused on offering a high level of security and legal clarity for all the products and services we offer; we believe only Bitcoin meets the high bar we have set. By being a Bitcoin-only platform, we can focus our energies on offering a high-quality product to our customers instead of fighting legal battles with SEC and other agencies. Not to mention, over the long term, we believe 70% of alt-coins will no longer exist.
